Our light-filled two room apartment with private garden is private and peaceful. It is 24 hour accessible, spacious and self-contained. Though part of our house, it is separated by a double brick wall and has its own entrance. Heating, cooling, internet and a laptop workspace are all included. The #58 tram is a 3 minute walk and we are very close to Melbourneâ€™s famous 181 hectare (446 acre) Royal Park.

Recently renovated, the lounge room has full length windows looking out to the private garden, beautiful timber floors and a stocked up bookshelf. The bedroom has wool carpet, a large built-in wardrobe and quality linen. The bathroom and kitchenette were fully renovated in 2019. While we are close to Grantham Street shops and tram line, and have very quick and easy access to the airport, we are in a really quiet spot.

Interaction with guests

Greta and Tony live at the property. We are around weeknights, weekends and in the evenings to provide directions or advice on places to go and things to do. Once we have introduced ourselves we will just get on with our lives. We are more than happy to help you out and have a chat, or leave you to relax and enjoy the space in private.

About us

Greta and Tony work in education and health respectively. We enjoy all that Brunswick has to offer especially the cafes, pubs, live music and the tremendous variety of restaurants. When at home we live a pretty quiet life and enjoy trying our hand at every sort of cuisine, our current obsession is the food of Yotam Ottolenghi. Our adult children and friends drop in for coffee, wine and dinner.
