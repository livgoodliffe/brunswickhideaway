About

Our light-filled two room apartment with private garden a peaceful hideaway. It is 24 hour accessible, spacious and self-contained. It is attached to our house by a double brick wall, and has its own entrance. Heating, cooling, internet and a laptop workspace are all included.

Recently renovated, the lounge room has full length windows looking out to the garden, beautiful timber floors and a stocked up bookshelf. The bedroom has wool carpet, a large built-in wardrobe and quality linen. We are close to Grantham Street shops and tram line, and have very quick and easy access to the airport.

Location

Brunswick

Brunswick is a multicultural suburb 4km from the city centre. It has a vast array of restaurants ranging from vegan to middle-eastern, lots of artist run galleries, hip cafes, live music, even an African jazz bar. Our house has really quick and easy access to Tullamarine Freeway with the advantage of no freeway noise.

Hosts

Greta and Tony live at the property. We are around weeknights, weekends and in the evenings to provide directions or advice on places to go and things to do. Once we have introduced ourselves we will just get on with our lives. We are more than happy to help you out and have a chat, or leave you to relax and enjoy the space in private.

About us

Greta and Tony work in education and health respectively. We enjoy all that Brunswick has to offer especially the cafes, pubs, live music and the tremendous variety of restaurants. When at home we live a pretty quiet life and enjoy trying our hand at every sort of cuisine, our current obsession is the food of Yotam Ottolenghi. Our adult children and friends drop in for coffee, wine and dinner.
